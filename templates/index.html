About the Latin Contest

The Latin Contest is a national celebration of classical learning. Students demonstrate mastery of lingua Latina across reading comprehension, grammar, culture, and myth while developing confidence and community with fellow classicists.

Events are offered for multiple divisions (Novice through Advanced), with equitable rubrics and optional enrichment workshops led by faculty and alumni. Schools may participate on-site or online with secure proctoring.

Program Highlights

Designed with teachers, the contest emphasizes fair assessment and joyful scholarship. Expect:

  • Multiple divisions: Novice, Intermediate, Advanced
  • Culture & civilization, myth, etymology, grammar
  • Optional certamen-style team round
  • Teacher resources, sample tests, and rubric transparency

Eligibility

Open to students in grades 6–12 (or international equivalent). Schools, homeschool groups, and independent learners may participate with a registered proctor.

Divisions

Novice (Latin I), Intermediate (Latin II–III), Advanced (Latin IV+ / AP).

Teams or Individuals

Compete solo and optionally join a certamen-style round with your team.

Proctoring

On-site or remote with secure monitoring. Accommodations available.

Awards

Medals & certificates by division, plus special myth & culture citations.
